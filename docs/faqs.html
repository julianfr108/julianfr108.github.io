How do I get started?

The first step is usually to schedule a free consultation. You can schedule directly using this link: https://calendly.com/astateofmind

The consultation will help each of us to decide if I am a good fit for your needs. If we both agree it is a good fit, we can then schedule our initial Intake Session together.

Learn more on our Psychedelic Therapy page.

What can I expect during my first session?

The first session is also called an Intake Session. Julian Royce has developed a unique Intake Process that covers 4 fundamental pillars of Health and Wellbeing. These include: Sleep, Exercise, Diet & Nutrition, and Social Relationships including family, friendships & communities you are a part of.

We will also go over your relevant health history, your past experiences with therapy, coaching and other related healing modalities and look at what worked for you and what did not work so well for you. We will go over your goals and intentions for your own growth and healing process.

Finally, we will make a plan to move forward to best support you in your unique journey.

What types of Therapy do I offer?

Julian is trained in multiple therapy and healing modalities. He currently offers:

  • Somatic (body based) therapy
  • Trauma therapy
  • Couple's Counseling
  • Psychedelic assisted Therapy
  • Transformational Coaching
  • Medicine Ceremonies

How long are Sessions?

Currently we offer sessions that are 30 minutes, 53 minutes, 75 minutes, 90 minutes and 120 minutes long.

  • Most common: 53 minutes
  • Ketamine assisted Sessions: typically 120 minutes
  • Couple's Counseling (together): typically 75 or 90 minutes
  • Couple's Counseling (individual): 53 minutes
  • Retreat days, Intensives and Psychedelic assisted therapy: can be longer

Do you do Virtual / Online sessions?

Yes, we offer in person sessions here at our office in downtown Boulder and we also offer virtual telehealth sessions as well as phone sessions.

Do you accept Insurance?

You should call your Insurance company and ask to get clear about what your insurance will cover in terms of Mental Health Treatment. Please message us to learn more about the possibility of using insurance to help cover the costs of Therapy.

Do you provide Superbills for Insurance reimbursement?

Yes, we are happy to provide you with a Superbill for reimbursement.

What is your cancellation policy?
