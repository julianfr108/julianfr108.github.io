Life Transitions

Support for navigating life's biggest changes

The Unknown Can Be Scary

There are periods in life where certain decisions can significantly alter our trajectory.

These choices can weigh on us. It is hard not to second guess oneself, in such an important decision. Many times, we aren't sure which direction to go, or what choice to make.

Facing a large life transition such as graduation, being fired, a break up, or feeling called to travel, and various other transitions, can be difficult.

Common Thoughts During Life Transitions

  • "What should I do?"
  • "What if I make the wrong choice?"
  • "What do I truly want to do?"
  • "What kind of life am I meant to be living?"
  • "I'm afraid of messing this up"
  • "I'm afraid of the unknown."

Confusion, uncertainty, doubt, fear of failure, of the unknown, of making a mistake - they can make such a period very challenging.

Finding Your Way Through

In such a difficult time, the right support can make all the difference in the world.

Therapy and coaching can help you through such transitions and get to the other side, and give you the strength to be able to step into the new phase of your life with confidence.

Together, we can work on:

  • Clarifying your values and priorities
  • Exploring your options with curiosity instead of fear
  • Building confidence in your decision-making
  • Processing the emotions that come with change
  • Creating a vision for your next chapter
